Colors Characters Planes Story
White is one of the five colors of mana in Magic. It is drawn from the plains and embodies the principles of morality and order. White seeks peace through structure.
Blue is one of the five colors of mana in Magic. It is drawn from islands and embodies the concepts of logic and technology and seeks perfection through knowledge.
Black is one of the five colors of mana in Magic. It is drawn from the power of swamps and embodies the principles of free will and amorality. Black seeks to increase its power by all possible means, including opportunism, murder, treason, and cruelty.
Red is one of the five colors of mana in Magic. It is drawn from the mountains and embodies the principles of impulse and chaos. Red seeks freedom through action.
Green is one of the five colors of mana in Magic. It is drawn from the power of forests and embodies the principles of instinct and interdependence. Green seeks growth through acceptance.
